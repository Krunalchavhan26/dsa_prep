# Math Tricks for DSA

## Gaussian Sum Formula

Sum of 0 to n = n \* (n + 1) / 2

Example:
0+1+2+3 = 6
3\*(3+1)/2 = 6 ✅

## When to use?

- Missing Number problem
- When you need sum of first n natural numbers
- Avoid looping 0 to n just to get sum
